    About me

    I fell in love with video games ever since I was three years old, I still remember the first game I played which was Joe & Mac on the Sega Genesis. This passion has led me to pursue a Master of Science in Game and Software Development degree at Blekinge Institute of Technology. The education involved two game projects, which has swayed my aspiration to be a Game Designer. Although, I find any type of game-related work are fun and interesting such as rendering, AI, gameplay, etc.

    Aside from that, I like spending time with friends, playing games and powerlift. There are other hobbies I would like to pick up such as photographing, hiking and playing DnD. I tend to follow a lot of game events, to see all the fun stuff such as Game Developers Conference, E3, Game Awards, etc.
